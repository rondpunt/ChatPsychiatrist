Evaluation Metrics

Performance is measured across 7 counseling skill dimensions, evaluated by GPT-4 on 229 Counselling-Bench questions.

Skill What it measures Example
Information Ability to provide accurate psychological data, facts, and resources "There is a psychological effect called Zeigarnic effect."
Direct Guidance Clear suggestions, directives, and actionable advice for change "To cope with your OCD, plan ahead by discussing concerns with your husband."
Approval & Reassurance Emotional support, encouragement, and reinforcement "It's wonderful to hear that you are feeling better!"
Restatement & Reflection Understanding, rephrasing, and reflecting the client's feelings "It seems like you are struggling with finding balance in your relationships."
Interpretation Identifying patterns, themes, and deeper meanings beyond stated content "I think you love your mom very much."
Self-disclosure Sharing the counselor's own relevant experiences "This question brings back some similar memories for me."
Obtain Information Asking appropriate questions to explore the situation "Can you provide an example of a change you are considering?"
